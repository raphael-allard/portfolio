My projects

SOONS

SOONS (previously "Pong") is a Progressive Web App that allows you to create spontaneous events with your friends. Coded at Le Wagon with the Ruby on Rails framework, I worked on it as main developer during six months. Here are the main features: a user can see events on the map (through Geocoding and Mapbox), create one, search and add friends (using PG Search), receive notifications in-app and instantly talk with guests (with Action Cable). If they are on Android, they can also receive push notifications on their smartphone (Using Service Worker). iOS users will receive those push notifications on Messenger. There are also small other features including the Wicked gem, Sweet Alert or Flatpickr.

Air Sup'O

Air Sup’O is an AirBnB website like but instead of rent houses or flats, people may hire superheroes’ services. I developed it at Le Wagon with the Ruby on Rails framework. Here are the main features: a user can create and propose his own services as superhero (through Simple Form), find one of them already online (thanks to PG Search and Mapbox) and rent it.
